## 1. Executive Summary

This report analyses the three leading e-commerce SaaS platforms operating in the Bangladesh market — **ZatiqEasy**, **BitCommerz**, and **eBitans** — and demonstrates why Ecomai's fundamentally different monetisation model creates a superior market entry strategy, stronger merchant retention, and a larger long-term revenue ceiling.

**Key finding:** All three incumbents rely on fixed monthly subscriptions. Ecomai replaces this with a zero-upfront, commission-only model that eliminates the #1 barrier to merchant adoption in price-sensitive South Asian markets.

## 5. Business Model Analysis

### 5.1 The Subscription Model (Competitors)

```
Merchant signs up → Pays ৳500–৳5,000/month → Gets a store
                                              ↓
                          Makes sales?  YES → Platform keeps same fee
                                       NO  → Platform keeps same fee
                                              Merchant churns after 2–3 months
```

**Problem:** The platform earns the same whether the merchant thrives or fails. There is no incentive to help merchants sell more. Revenue is capped at (subscribers × plan price).

**Churn risk:** Merchants who don't see ROI within 1–2 months cancel. The platform must constantly acquire new merchants to replace churners. This is expensive.

### 5.2 The Commission Model (Ecomai)

```
Merchant signs up → Pays ৳0 → Gets a store
                               ↓
                  Makes sales?  YES → Ecomai earns 5% of each sale
                                NO  → Ecomai earns ৳0
                                       Merchant has no reason to leave
```

**Advantage:** The merchant has zero reason to churn if they aren't selling — it costs them nothing. The moment they start selling, both parties earn. **Ecomai is incentivised to help merchants sell more**, because more merchant revenue = more platform revenue.

**Revenue ceiling:** Uncapped. A merchant doing ৳5,00,000/month generates ৳25,000/month for Ecomai — far more than any subscription plan. At scale, commission revenue grows with the ecosystem.

## 6. Market Entry Advantage

### 6.1 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

The single biggest challenge for all four platforms is acquiring merchants. Here is how the pitch differs:

| Platform | Merchant Pitch |
|----------|---------------|
| ZatiqEasy | "Start your online store for ৳500/month" |
| BitCommerz | "Create your free e-commerce (then pay ৳999+/month)" |
| eBitans | "Go online from ৳849/month" |
| **Ecomai** | **"Start selling for free. Pay nothing until you earn."** |

In a market where the average small seller earns ৳15,000–৳25,000/month and operates on thin margins, the psychological difference between "pay ৳500–৳850 before making a single sale" and "pay nothing until you earn" is enormous.

**Expected conversion lift:** A zero-cost entry point typically converts 3–5× higher on paid advertising campaigns, reducing CAC proportionally.

### 6.2 Retention / Churn

| Model | Why merchants leave |
|-------|---|
| Subscription | "I'm paying ৳1,000/month and barely selling" → Cancels in month 2–3 |
| Commission | "I'm paying nothing and not selling much yet" → Stays indefinitely. No financial pressure to leave. |

A merchant who stays on the platform — even dormant — is a future revenue source. On subscription platforms, that same merchant is already lost.

### 6.3 Word-of-Mouth Growth

Free platforms spread faster. A merchant paying ৳0 who makes their first sale will tell 5 other sellers. A merchant paying ৳849/month who hasn't sold anything yet will tell nobody.

## 8. What Ecomai Has That None of Them Have

### 8.1 Multi-Tenant Architecture
Ecomai is built as a true multi-tenant SaaS from day one. One codebase serves unlimited shops with complete data isolation. Competitors appear to run separate instances or WordPress-based setups per store.

### 8.2 Commission-Based Revenue Engine
The earnings system — with real-time commission calculation, shop balance tracking, withdrawal workflows, and adjustable commission rates — is a core platform feature, not a bolt-on. No competitor has this.

### 8.3 Super Admin Platform Oversight
A centralised super admin can view all shops, all orders, all revenue, all users from a single dashboard. This enables:
- Platform-level analytics for investor reporting
- Quick intervention when shops have issues
- Commission adjustment per shop or globally
- Future: automated fraud detection across the platform

### 8.4 AI-Powered Campaign Generation
Built-in marketing campaign generation using AI. Merchants describe what they want to promote, the system generates campaign copy. None of the three competitors offer this.

### 8.5 Path to Seller Financing
Because Ecomai processes every transaction and knows each merchant's real-time sales velocity, it has the **data infrastructure** to offer revenue-based financing (like ZatiqEasy's Capital product) but with superior underwriting data — because the platform sees every order, not just self-reported numbers.
